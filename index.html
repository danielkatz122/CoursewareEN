Courseware

Courseware is an interactive method through a computer or tablet for imparting new knowledge and skills. With the help of this tool, you can transfer new information, practice, and progress at the pace of the learner, and you can also move forward and backward in order to repeat the information. Today there are many types of coursewares: A courseware to learn a language, to transfer mandatory content, to practice, an exam, and more.
